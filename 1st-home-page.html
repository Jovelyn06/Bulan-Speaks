Bulan Speaks

Bulan Speaks is a platform dedicated to allowing the Bulaneño community to report issues and share concerns about their selected barangay. Users can also rate services such as healthcare, infrastructure, projects, and many more. This provides valuable insight into the challenges faced by the community. Bulan Speaks also serves as a progress tracker, offering a way to track the progress of improvements through ongoing reviews. We compile user reports and send them directly to the local government units (LGUs), ensuring the community's voices are heard and promoting greater transparency and accountability. Through this platform, we aim to give the community a space where they can report issues, speak their concerns, and encourage them to speak up for a better community and positive change.

Together, we can strive for a better Bulan!
